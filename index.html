heyDJ is a mobile app that allows the user to follow and search through their favorite Dj's

Find & Follow

Tons of local and Industry Dj's at your fingertips

The heyDj app allows you to search and follow your favorite Dj's profiles.
Dj profiles allow you to search their music catalogue to request a song and tip,
gives you access to the current playing song, and upcoming events.

What Ya Wanna Hear?

Party to the perfect song to fit your mood

Have you ever been to a party and really wanted to hear a certain song? Now you can check if the Dj can play it.
Search through the available music catalogue and find the song you want to hear.

Tip the Dj

They'll greatly appreciate it

The heyDj app gives you the option to include a tip with your song request. Your account will only be deducted if your request has been granted.
